Practical Information

API Documentation: Clicking the API documentation button will download a PDF containing clear instructions on how to use the API.

Frontend: This frontend is limited to the GET methods of our API. Indeed, for the remaining methods, primarily intended for creators, we believe it is necessary not to oversimplify the creation of NFTs. We want creators to understand what they are doing.

Importance of Education: For us, it is crucial to educate oneself daily and understand what one is using. That's why we encourage our users to explore what happens on the blockchain when they create an NFT collection or acquire certain ones (using tools like Etherscan and OpenSea).
